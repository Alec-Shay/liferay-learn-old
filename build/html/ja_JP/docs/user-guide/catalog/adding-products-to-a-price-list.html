Adding Products to a Price List¶

Once you have a price list created, you can add products to it. This article describes two ways to add products to a price list. Prices that are set via a Price List will take precedence over prices set directly against a given SKU for the associated users.

Adding Products to a Price List Through the Price Lists Menu¶

When adding a large number of existing products to a new price list, populate the price list quickly using the following method:

  1. Navigate to the Control Panel → Commerce → Price Lists.

  2. Click on a price list (VIP Customers in this example).

  3. Click the Entries sub-tab.

  4. Click the (+) button.

  5. Choose one or more products (Premium Brake Fluid and Premium Brake Pads in this example) to be added to the price list.

  6. Click Add.

  7. Click on a product (Premium Brake Fluid in this example).

  8. Enter the following fields:

    • Price: 100

    • Promo Price: 95

  9. Click Save.

The product is now added to the price list. Repeat as necessary.

Adding Products to a Price List Through the Products Menu¶

When creating a new product, you can easily add it to an existing price list using this method:

  1. Navigate to the Control Panel → Commerce → Products.

  2. Click on a product (Premium Brake Fluid in this example).

  3. Click the SKUs sub-tab.

  4. Click on a SKU (MIN93017)

  5. Click Price Lists.

  6. Click the (+) button.

  7. Choose one or more price lists (VIP Customers in this example) to which this SKU will be added.

  8. Click Add.

  9. Click on a price list (VIP Customers in this example).

  10. Enter the following fields (example values shown below):

    • Price: 100
    • Promo Price: 95

  11. Click Save.

The product (Premium Brake Fluid) is now added to the price list (VIP Customers). Repeat the steps to add additional products to this price list or another list.
